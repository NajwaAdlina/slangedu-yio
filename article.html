What is slang?

Slang

In Indonesian, slang refers to colloquial language. Slang is typically used by members of social groups who have close relationships, such as close friends.

According to Britannica website, slang is a collection of words or expressions that are not commonly used in general to convey new ideas or old concepts in a different way. It is not only casual but also tends to be informal, sometimes even considered rude or vulgar. Therefore, the use of slang is generally limited to people or groups who have close relationships, as it is informal and unofficial.

Furthermore, slang is often encountered on almost all social media platforms today. Whether teenagers or adults, they use slang when talking or commenting on things or content they see. Of course, this attracts many people to find out and learn every slang that appears on social media. One example is the use of words like "slay" or "FOMO" that are frequently encountered in online interactions.

Here are some examples of slang that you may have heard before,

1.Lit

Lit is an abbreviation of "literally" which literally means something very interesting or cool.

Contoh:"The party last night was so lit! We danced until morning."

Artinya: "Pesta tadi malam sangat luar biasa! Kami menari sampai pagi."

2.Bae

Bae is an abbreviation of "Before Anyone Else," usually used to refer to someone who is the most important or special person in someone's life.

Contoh:"Spending the weekend with my bae is always the best part of my week."

Artinya: "Menghabiskan liburan akhir pekan bersama kekasih selalu menjadi bagian yang paling menyenangkan dalam minggu saya."

3.Spilled the tea

Surely you often come across this on social media, right? Or maybe you are one of those who use this slang? Well, usually, spilled the tea is used when someone gives or reveals gossip or the latest information, especially related to personal life or recent developments.

Contoh:"She really spilled the tea about what happened at the party last night!"

Artinya: "Dia benar-benar menceritakan gosip tentang apa yang terjadi di pesta semalam!"
